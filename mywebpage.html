Broccoli salad

2 bunches of broccoli, florets only (5-6 Cups)

  • 1 medium onion, chopped.
  • 1/2 cup of rasins
  • 1/2 cup of sunflower seeds 6-8 slices of bacon, fired and crumbled/li>

prep

  1. seperate florets from broccoli stalks
  2. combine salad indredients
  3. top with dressing
  4. chill and serve
