    Sweet ginger carrots

    Ingredients

    • 3 lbs of carrots peeled and sliced
    • 1/4 cup butter
    • 3 scallions, chopped 1 tbsp of grated ginger 1/4 cup of honey 1/4 cup of thawd OJ 1/2 tsp of thyme, dried and crushed

    prep

    1. steam carrots for 8 mins
    2. melt butter over medium heat, add onions and ginger. Cook and stir for 2 mins, add honey OJ and thyme
    3. pour mixture over carrots, and toss to coat.
    4. serve warm, serves 12
